Product Description

The Cannon 1230 uses a 20.1MP Type-1 sensor like its predecessor. However, it has an ultrawide 20mm lens and allows for 2x digital zoom at 1080p and 1.5x at 4K. There is no built-in optical stabilization, but the camera has electronic stabilization. However, turning it on will limit the zoom to a 1.23x crop.
Monitor: 7.5 cm TFT LCD panel (flip-out)
Connectivity:  Bluetooth 4.1, Wi-Fi, NFC
Port:   USB Type-C, microSD card, 3.5mm, Mini HDMI
Storage media: SD memory card
Battery: 60 min recording
Weight: 256 grams
Colour option: black and white
